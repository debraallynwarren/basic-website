Bernie

Our Gentle Giant

Bernie, a stray who "timed-out" at a kill shelter, was first saved by the Longmont Humane Society. We adopted him from them after he had been there for about a month.

The shelter wasn't sure what kind of dog he was, and we think he was a Catahoula Leopard mix. He was a 75 pound gentle soul who's feelings hurt easily and he took as much affection as he could get.

Bernie was a master on a leash and a perfect walking companion if the walk was slow. If we stopped, he stopped and if we stopped for too long he sat down.

Bernie loved to sleep, but his favorite thing to do was to eat french fries.
